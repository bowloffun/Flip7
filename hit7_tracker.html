How to Play Hit

Objective: Accumulate the highest score by drawing cards without busting.

Setup: Use 3 standard decks combined (11 copies of Ace-5, 7 copies of 6-10).

Gameplay: On your turn, draw cards one at a time. If you match a card you already have and have 3+ cards, you bust and lose all cards in front of you.

Stealing: If a card matches another player's card, you can steal all copies from that playerâ€”but watch out! This adds to your bust risk.

Banking: When your turn ends and you don't bust, all cards bank safely to your victory pile.

Scoring: Cards = face value (Ace=1). Highest banked total wins!
